Instructional Connections

Giving educators the freedom to choose from the widest range of learning tools

Don’t get locked into one-size-fits-all content. When you choose MAP® Growth™, you’re free to choose the supplemental resources that best meet the needs of your students, including some of the most popular instructional resources you may already be using.

When you share MAP Growth data with any of the 30+ providers below, you help save valuable instructional time by reducing assessments and valuable teacher time by making differentiated learning easier!

How it works

Getting started

Contact the instructional resource provider to understand how they receive MAP Growth data. If the provider can receive MAP Growth data automatically, here are the steps for how to share data.

Meet MAP Accelerator

NWEA® partnered with Khan Academy® to develop MAP Accelerator™ to help teachers unlock learning gains for every student. This powerful tool ties MAP Growth instructional area scores to personalized learning pathways so students get practice tailored to their needs, whether on, above, or below grade level.
